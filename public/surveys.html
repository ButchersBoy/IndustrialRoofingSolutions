Roof Survey Report Overview

A roof survey report provides a comprehensive analysis of the condition of a roof, highlighting any existing issues and recommending necessary actions. This report is essential for property owners, tenants and contractors to make informed decisions regarding maintenance, repairs or replacements.

Key Components of a Roof Survey Report

Introduction

  • Purpose of the survey
  • Overview of the property and roof type
  • Description of the inspection techniques used (e.g. drone technology, visual inspections)
  • Date and duration of the survey

    • Visual Findings

  • Detailed observations of the condition of the roof
  • High-resolution images and videos from the survey
  • Identification of areas needing attention (e.g. leaks, damage, wear)

    • Recommendations

  • Suggested repairs or maintenance tasks
  • Estimated timelines for completion
  • Potential cost implications

    • Conclusion

  • Summary of key findings
  • Overall assessment of the roofs condition

    Risk Mitigation

    Ensures the roof is in optimal condition, preserving property value.
