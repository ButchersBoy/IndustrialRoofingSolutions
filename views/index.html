Industrial Roofing Solutions

Industrial Roofing Solutions is a roofing
company based in Birmingham, UK.

We have a vast amount of experience and knowledge in roofing and cladding. Our team specialises in roof sheeting, cladding, flat roofs, new build, leaks and repairs, roof light repairs and replacement, blocked downpipes, asbestos maintenance and removal, gutter repairs and maintenance, storm damage, emergency repairs and annual maintenance. We will also arrange plant hire, netting, steel erecting and scaffolding. We have been working ONLY on industrial roofs for 20 years.
