a few words about us

INDUSTRIAL ROOFING SOLUTIONS are a local roofing company with a vast amount of experience and local knowledge in industrial roofing and cladding. Our team specialises in roof sheeting, cladding, flat roofs, new build, leaks and repairs, roof light repairs and replacement, blocked downpipes, asbestos maintenance and removal, gutter repairs and maintenance, storm damage, emergency repairs and annual maintenance. We will also arrange plant hire, netting, steel erecting and scaffolding. We have been working ONLY on industrial roofs for 20 years.

IRS has an extensive client base and has undertaken many projects throughout the Midlands. Some of these include works for Birmingham International Airport, Renault, NHS, Glendale and Asda.

We fully recognise the health and safety regulations and laws as they change year to year and our staff is constantly trained in safe working procedures. We understand to the need to produce risk assessments and method statements prior to any job commencing. We are also fully insured against public liability, professional indemnity and contract works. All our operatives have the correct certification for the use of heavy plant such as scissor lifts, cherry pickers and all terrain tele-handlers. . All our operatives have all been trained in the removal of asbestos contained roofing products, this allows us to strip your asbestos cement roof and have it removed from site by licensed carriage. Our operatives have also been security screened and have disclosures within the last 3 months. All this information can be found in our safety file or is available for immediate inspection.

A member of our team can come out and offer you free quotes or advice on what method would best suit your roofing scenario.
